Potato City Information

About Our Underground Community

🏘️ City Districts

Potato City has 5 main districts organized by size and specialty:

  1. 🍟 French Fry District - The bustling commercial center
  2. 🥔 Mashed Potato Heights - Upscale residential area
  3. 🍠 Sweet Potato Suburbs - Family-friendly neighborhoods
  4. 🥨 Chip Valley - Industrial and manufacturing zone
  5. ✨ Crispy Hills - Scenic recreational area

🎯 Popular Activities

Things to do in Potato City:

  • 🏛️ Visit the Underground Museum
  • 🎪 Attend the Harvest Festival
  • 🌱 Walk in Sprouting Gardens
  • 🛍️ Shop at Root Cellar Mall
  • 🎬 Watch movies at Tuber Theater

🎓 Educational Institutions

Schools in our city:

  1. 📚 Potato Elementary School
  2. 📖 Fries Middle School
  3. 🎓 Yukon High School

All schools feature state-of-the-art underground facilities and specialized agricultural programs.

🌱
